DK's Perfect Steak

Ready for the best steak of your life in under 30 minutes?

Ingredients

  • Steak, 1 to 1-1/2 inch thick cuts recomended
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Start by seasoning the steak with salt. As the steak rests and comes down to room temperature, start the grill or smoker and set temperature to 450 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Once steak has warmed to room temperature, pat dry and season with pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  3. After the grill has come to 450 degrees, place the steaks on the grill, and cook for four to four and a half minutes per side.
  4. Cook times may vary slightly depending on the grill and size of cuts, but remove at the recommended time for a medium-rare cook.
  5. Let the steak rest for at least 5 minutes before cutting to ensure ti reabsorbes the juices.
  6. Garnish and enjoy.
