New York Eggs

These eggs are sure to make you rethink what scrembled eggs should be!

Ingredients

  • Farm Fresh Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cream Cheese
  • American Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Crack eggs and combine with milk in a small bowl. Add one table spoon of milk per egg used.
  2. Whisk with for until mixed, and add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Heat pan with butter on medium heat. Add eggs to the pan once the butter is melted and pan is hot.
  4. Cook eggs until just before fully cooked, stirring occasionally.
  5. Lower heat, and add 1 slice of american cheese and a tablespoon of cream cheese per 2 eggs.
  6. When cheese is melted, remove from pan, garnish, and enjoy!
